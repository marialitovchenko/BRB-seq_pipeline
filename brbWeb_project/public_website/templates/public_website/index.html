About us

Alithea Genomics provides a full range of services in the domain of molecular biology and bioinformatics.

MERCURIUS technology

Our proprietary technology called MERCURIUS makes it possible to perform RNA-seq on a large number of samples in a fast, robust and cost-effective way.

Bioinformatics services

Perform an analysis of your MERCURIUS data with just couple of cliks on our server

Full range of service

You can rely on us on all stages of your experiment: from RNA extraction to bioinformatics analysis

Services

Currently we are offering two types of services:

3' mRNA sequencing service

Send us your tissue, cells or RNA samples and let us perform RNA-seq for you. Thanks to MERCURIUS, we will be able to process your samples at a fraction of the cost of other service providers.

MERCURIUS kit

Try out our kits and experience in first person the convenience of MERCURIUS. Each kit can be used to perform MERCURIUS on 96 samples.
